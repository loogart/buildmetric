About

Why is Buildmetric unique?

We finally reached that point as a builder that we knew our manual workaround approach to managing build deficiencies was not longer going to cut it. So we checked out the various Construction-focused ERP software out there. They had lots of functionality, but were way to bloated and required way too much onboarding.

So we looked for something easier to use, like Punchlist software, but they lacked the breadth of functionality we needed. Then we found BuildMetric and we now have the best of both worlds – a complete solution that is easy to use.
